Our Team

CREATE YOUR SPACE WITH OUR DESIGNERS

Meet our global community of talented designers who are just as passionate about bringing exceptional designs to the masses.

Abigail Johnson

Abigail Johnson is an Australian industrial designer based in Sydney, who started a workshop, ARGO in the 1990s to develop experimental furniture and objects.

Mary Barra

Mary Barra's work is focused on finding intelligent, human-centred solutions that exceed the obvious & attempt to offer a fresh and observational approach to given problem.

Ginni Rometty

Created with Italian designer, the Strato collection is complete with gentle curves and distinctive walnut-and-black, lending a graceful touch to any room.

James Harrison

As part of Castlery’s international designer series, the Lily collection by British James Harrison features sleek silhouettes juxtaposed with striking chevron patterns.
